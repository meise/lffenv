I wanna help!

If that is what you are thinking right now, congratulations, then you have understood an important part of Freifunk, the community aspect :). A community project is only as great as the engagement behind it.

So far we are mostly very geeky guys and are therefore wacky by law ;). We can always need people making flyers and promoting Freifunk in general, people that are willing to give us access to rooftops on high buildings (chruch towers are perfect to transmit miles away :) ) or are good at crafting and can mount antennas/routers on rooftops. Of course also donations for new devices are very welcome.

On the technical side we can need people who have some experience with IPv6, Linux/OpenWRT or BATMAN-Advanced, as well as radio hams that have experience with antennas.
