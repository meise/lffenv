Freifunk - I'm interested, what now?

You can get a quite good signal from the window in your kitchen but not on the couch in your living room? You are welcome to borrow a preconfigured router for 30€ which will boost the signal and enlarge the mesh cloud. You only have to place it at your window and plug it in the socket. If you are interested, just drop us an eMail (freifunk.luebeck@asta.uni-luebeck.de).

You are also very welcome to stop by our weekly CCCHL meetings in the city center, it would be a pleasure for us to explain everything about the Freifunk project to you.
