An open mesh-network for everyone by everyone

The idea behind Freifunk is to create an autonomous, wireless network with the efforts of a community, a project pushed by similar principles as the Wikipedia community for instance. Everyone shall have the chance to enlarge the "WLAN-cloud" by putting up a mesh-router to support a free, independent and open network - and to use it.

Freifunk

The whole idea is based on the Freifunk project initiated in Berlin, which is already having more than 300 routers running. That became possible because of the many volunteers contributing to this community project. Freifunk is based on the following principles:
• Access to knowledge and public information should be free as in freedom
• Nobody should restrict communication with others
• Local and global wireless and wired networks should be public spaces like streets, parcs, forests and the sea
• Networks and digital infrastructures must be based on free and open source software and open standards

Services

Internet

For one thing you can use the mesh network to get internet access at no charge. With the following rules:
• No warranty - We are not a commercial internet service provider
• Be nice to your neighbours - Do not do anything that could harm your neighbour

Intranet

Some services are planned which will only be reachable over the mesh network itself. (give the chat software Pidgin with Bonjour a shot - http://pidgin.im)
